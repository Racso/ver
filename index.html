Ship dates,
not numbers.

Your build timestamp is your version number. Automatic, monotonic, and meaningful.

Most software isn't a library. A game, a desktop app, a mobile app — they don't have "backwards-compatible APIs." Yet teams still burn time deciding whether the next build is 1.4.2 or 1.5.0.

The questions teams actually ask are about time: "Is this the latest build?" "Does this include yesterday's fix?" "Which came first?" Datetime versioning answers those questions directly. All timestamps are UTC.

The schemes

SunVer
The version is the date. No encoding.
2026.417.830
resolution 1 min legibility perfect MSI no
MoonVer
SunVer within MSI limits. 10-minute resolution.
26.4.17083
resolution 10 min legibility high MSI yes
BinVer
Opaque packed timestamp. Maximum precision.
1.65.9574
resolution 1 sec legibility none MSI yes
